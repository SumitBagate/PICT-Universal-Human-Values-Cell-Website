About UHV Cell

Universal Human Values Cell - PICT Initiative

The Universal Human Values (UHV) Cell at Pune Institute of Computer Technology is an AICTE-based initiative dedicated to fostering ethical values, social responsibility, and holistic development among students and faculty.

Human Values Integration

Integrating human values across academic curricula and co-curricular activities for comprehensive student development.

Faculty Development

Conducting workshops and training programs to enhance faculty awareness of values education and mentoring methodologies.

Student Empowerment

Empowering students through mentorship programs, ethical discussions, and community service activities.

Social Responsibility

Promoting social responsibility and creating awareness about environmental sustainability and ethical practices.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To nurture students and faculty with universal human values, ethical awareness, empathy, responsibility, and a commitment to meaningful contribution in academics, profession, and society.

The UHV Cell aims to create a learning environment where character development grows alongside technical excellence.

Vision

To build a compassionate, value-based academic community that develops responsible engineers, thoughtful leaders, and socially aware citizens.

The vision is to integrate human values into every stage of education so that students grow into balanced individuals who serve the nation and humanity with integrity.

Our Objectives

Faculty Development Programs

Comprehensive workshops and training sessions to enhance faculty expertise in values-based education and mentoring techniques.

Student Workshops

Interactive sessions on ethics, integrity, communication skills, leadership, and personal development for holistic growth.

Mentorship Programs

Structured mentoring initiatives connecting experienced faculty and industry professionals with students for guidance and support.

Ethical Education

Promoting ethical decision-making, moral values, and responsible behavior through classroom discussions and case studies.

Co-curricular Activities

Organizing seminars, debates, and cultural events that promote teamwork, creativity, and social awareness among students.

Volunteer Activities

Community service initiatives and social outreach programs fostering responsibility and compassion among students.
